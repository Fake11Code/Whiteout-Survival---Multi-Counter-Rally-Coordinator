How to Coordinate Multiple Counters

  1. Select the number of players who will participate in the counter attack
  2. Set the enemy rally parameters - launch time and march duration
  3. Choose a counter strategy:
    • Simultaneous: All counters arrive at the same time (maximizes damage)
    • Staggered: Counters arrive 5 seconds apart (more forgiving)
    • Custom: Set custom arrival delays for each player
  4. Calculate timings to get launch times for all players
  5. Share the launch times with your alliance members
  6. Use the countdown timers to coordinate perfectly
  7. Mark as launched when each player sends their rally

Multi-Counter Strategy Tips

  • 1 Player: Good for testing enemy defenses or quick recapture
  • 2 Players: One breaks defenses, the other secures the castle
  • 3 Players: Overwhelming force with backup in case of misses
  • 4 Players: Maximum power - almost guaranteed success
  • Assign roles: First counter weakens, follow-ups secure the castle
  • Use in-game chat to confirm when each player has launched
  • Have a backup plan if someone misses their timing
